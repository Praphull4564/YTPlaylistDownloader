Why Authentication is Needed

YouTube has anti-bot measures that require authentication for downloading some videos and playlists. You need to provide your YouTube cookies to bypass these restrictions.

Export Cookies Manually

Export cookies from your browser using an extension, then upload the file.

Install a Cookie Exporter Extension

Install one of these extensions in your browser:

  • Get cookies.txt (Chrome/Edge)
  • Cookies.txt (Firefox)
2
Log in to YouTube

Make sure you're logged into YouTube in your browser.

3
Export Cookies

Click on the extension icon while on YouTube and export the cookies file.

4
Upload the Cookies File

Return to the main page and upload the cookies file when downloading a playlist.
